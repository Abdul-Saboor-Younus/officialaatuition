Introduction

Our specialised teachers are on-hand to give your child the confidence and enjoyment they need to excel in their learning. AA Tuition Centre is a successful learning centre run by professional tutors. Our strong reputation has been built upon successful results from our past and present students. Learning is an on-going process throughout life, and at AA Tuition Centre, we strongly believe that children learn faster through enjoyment of the subject. As requirements on young people continue to increase, supportive guidance by friendly, experienced tutors has never been so important.

At AA Tuition Centre our tutors will get to know your child as an individual. Whether they are finding school tricky or top of the class, we will create a learning plan designed to help them to gain confidence, enthusiasm, and new skills. We are renowned for our outstanding results at all key stages of the curriculum and in particular at Matric and Intermediate Level. We believe that when pupils are taught in a small group they tend to become more confident and learn to compete against one another. Working alongside their counterparts the pupil becomes more focused and determined to be at top of the class. Children often benefit from each others comprehension and become more open to different styles of learning. At AA Tuition Centre, our aim is give every child the best possible chance of increasing their understanding of Maths, Science, Commerce, Computer, Programming Languages and English.

We can prepare your child for forthcoming exams to alleviate stress and give them the confidence they need to achieve their goals. We have had great success with our current pupils. Our inspiring and highly experienced tutors provide students with carefully designed educational programmes, put together specifically to meet their individual needs. We believe all children have enormous and often untapped potential and that, through developing strong maths and English skills, they can experience all round academic success. We therefore believe all education must focus on delivering a maximum, long-term benefit to each individual child

Develop skills and confidence in Maths and English that will give your child the best head start in life. Register your child now. At AA Tuition Centre we care about each individual child and giving them the opportunity to succeed in the global community.

    At AA Tuition Centre we can help with:
  • Learning difficult subjects for students of all abilities
  • Excellent exam preparation (stress management, revision & techniques)
  • Achieving higher grades and increasing students confidence
  • Encouragement, support and guidance
  • Homework (feedback provided)
